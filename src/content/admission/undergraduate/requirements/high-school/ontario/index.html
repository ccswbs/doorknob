Minimum requirements for all Ontario-based applicants

Undergraduate degree program applicants from Ontario must present the following:

  1. Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  2. A minimum of six 4U and/or 4M courses
  3. The Bachelor Degree or Associate Diploma Requirements
  4. ENG4U (required for all degree programs)

NOTE: The non-academic component of 4U or 4M co-op courses is not accepted for admission purposes.

Things to remember:

  • Estimated cutoff ranges are based on admission averages from previous years and are provided as a point of reference.
  • Majors with a single asterisk * indicates that the program is offered with and without co-op.
  • Majors with a double asterisk ** indicates that the program has a mandatory co-op.
