## Program Requirements
Applicants from Ontario must present the following:

- Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
- A minimum of six 4U and/or 4M courses
- ENG4U
- The Specific Subject Requirements for the program of interest.

The non-academic component of 4U or 4M Co-op courses is not accepted for admission purposes.
