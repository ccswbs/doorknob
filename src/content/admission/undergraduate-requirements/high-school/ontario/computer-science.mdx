## Subject Specific Requirements

In addition to the general admission requirements for all programs, this program requires that applicants present the following subject prerequisites:

- English (ENG4U)
- Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)
- 4 additional 4U or 4M courses

### Estimated Averages Cut-off Range

**87-91%**

Applicants opting for the co-op variant of this major should note that co-op averages normally exceed the estimated cut-off ranges.
